Astrological Signs

The first element of astrology to learn, and the one many people tend to be familiar with, are astrological signs! Starting with the basics, each sign is associated with a modality, element, and planet, resulting in 12 different “archetypes” with varying characteristics/themes. Before diving in to each sign, let’s first break down the two of the qualities assigned:

Elements
Signs are broken into 4 elements: fire, water, earth, and air. Each element represents a basic temperament:

Elements in Astrology
Fire Earth Air Water
Spontaneous, impulsive, quick to emotional response Reliable, practical, grounded Intellectual, objective, fair-minded Intuitive, sensitive, deeply emotional, and nurturing
Dynamic, passionate, spirited; tend to take the lead Cautious, focused, and appreciative of material possessions Communicative and perceptive, very social Rely more on feeling than logic
Signs include Aries, Sagittarius, and Leo Signs include Virgo, Taurus, and Capricorn Signs include Aquarius, Libra, and Gemini Signs include Scorpio, Pisces, and Cancer

Modalities
Modalities are basically different “states” each element can exist in. It can represent the behavioral approach you tend to take.

Astrology Modalities
Fixed Cardinal Mutable
Determined, sometimes to a point of stubbornness Quick, ambitious, and active Often seek (and find it easy to) change
Have a hard time changing or leaning away from their own approach Love to start new ideas/projects, but often move to another before finishing, the ‘initiator’ modality Very adaptable to any situation, extremely resourceful
Patiently and steadily work towards goals Very focused on their own goals, can lose sight of others Approach others with diplomacy
Signs include Scorpio, Leo, Taurus, and Aquarius Signs include Libra, Capricorn, Cancer, and Aries Signs include Sagittarius, Gemini, Virgo, and Pisces

When we put it all together, a sign tells us the personality or energy one may carry, and in what way that personality is shown. For example, Libra and Aquarius are both air signs; they both tend to rely more on intellect than emotion and can be very social. However, an Aquarius may be more likely to stick it out through obstacles, or be stubborn in their views, while a Libra may quickly switch to a new project as well as view. With these concepts in mind, let’s start breaking down each individual sign!

Aries

  • Always looking for a challenge, very competitive
  • Full of energy and life, to an impulsive degree at times
  • Can have a hot temper, but move past things quickly and stay optimistic

Taurus

  • More than willing to work hard for things that will make them happy
  • Loves comfort, relaxation, and beautiful things
  • Doesn’t lose temper easily, but can hold a long grudge once it happens

Gemini

  • Love of sharing ideas with others, communication is of great importance
  • Generally very social with a wide variety of friends and interests
  • Can easily make new friends because they adapt to new situations and conversation topics well

Cancer

  • Known for their compassion and almost motherly level of nurturing
  • Tend to be very sensitive or moody
  • Wants to be needed by others and is deeply loyal to loved ones

Leo

  • Deeply important for them to have time to do what they want, gives them joy, and allows free expression
  • Always think big, but bored by details
  • Love to have an audience and feel the attention on them

Virgo

  • Gentle, quiet, and organized
  • Always ready to help others, service is an important moral value to them
  • Have a love for all things both beautiful and useful

Libra

  • Harmony and balance drive their actions
  • Skilled at diplomacy, seeing all sides of an issue, and debate
  • Very relationship oriented and crave a unified community

Scorpio

  • Naturally intuitive, secretive, reserved, and can pick up others’ character quickly
  • Feel very deeply and have great inner awareness
  • Drawn to intensity and can be intense themselves

Sagittarius

  • Happiest when traveling, not tied down to a routine, or starting something new
  • Restless and adventurous, always want to experience as much life as possible
  • Bring lively energy everywhere they go

Capricorn

  • Known for their self-discipline and tireless work ethic
  • Important for them to be good at something, to feel success and recognition
  • Have a hard time seeking help, but are incredibly resilient

Aquarius

  • Intelligent, forward-thinkers, unafraid to speak their mind
  • Known for cleverness and dry sense of humor
  • Naturally curious, able to stay objective; humanitarians

Pisces

  • Sympathetic and receptive; believe in the good of others
  • Retain a sense of innocence their whole lives, but described as an “old soul”
  • Have a rich inner world and vivid imagination
